Yummy Yards helps Vancouver residents grow food in the ultimate local location: your home! 

Whether you are looking to have vegetable plot in your yard, want to add some edible plants to your garden or would just like to learn more about growing things to eat, we can help!

Installation

Installing a new garden can be a daunting task for anyone, let our experienced hands help out   with the set-up of your edible garden
  • creating garden beds and building raised beds
  • amending soil
  • planting
  • setting up irrigation

Design

We'll work with you in designing an edible garden that works for you
  • choosing favourite vegetables, fruit, flowers and herbs that will thrive in your yard
  • finding an ideal location and size for your vegetable garden
  • planning crop rotations and beneficial companion plantings
  • finding a balance of practical food growing and aesthetic value in your garden that suits your needs

Maintenance

Don’t have the time to maintain or work on your garden?  We can provide regular maintenance throughout the season so that all you have to do, is enjoy the fresh, yummy, bounty produced two steps from your doorstep!
  • weeding
  • harvesting
  • replanting
  • irrigation
